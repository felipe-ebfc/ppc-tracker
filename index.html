What is PPC%?

Percent Plan Complete — Last Planner System

Percent Plan Complete (PPC%) is the core metric of the Last Planner System® in Lean Construction. It measures how reliably a team keeps its weekly commitments.

PPC% = Completed ÷ Planned × 100

Each week, the team lists all tasks they commit to completing — not just what they'll try, but what they promise to deliver. At week's end, each task is marked done or not done.

When a task isn't completed, record a variance reason — understanding why plans fail is just as important as tracking when they fail.

85%+Excellent
70–84%Good
<70%Improve

Consistently tracking PPC% drives team accountability, surfaces systemic obstacles, and creates a culture of reliable commitments on the jobsite.
