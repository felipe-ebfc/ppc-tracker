What is PPC%

Percent Plan Complete — Last Planner System

Percent Plan Complete (PPC%) is the core metric of the Last Planner System® in Lean Construction. It measures how reliably a team keeps its weekly commitments.

PPC% = Completed ÷ Planned × 100

Each week, Last Planners list all commitments they promise to complete. At week’s end, each is marked Completed or not. When a commitment isn’t kept, record the variance reason — understanding why plans fail is just as important as tracking when they fail.

80%+Excellent
60–79%Improve
<60%Critical

Teams that honestly track variance patterns typically see PPC jump from 50–60% to 75–85% within months. PPC without variance tracking is just blame.
